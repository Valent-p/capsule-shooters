# Location: Pitex Station - Maintenance Annex (First Level)

## Overview
The player awakens in the Maintenance Annex of Pitex Station, the starting point of their rebellion. The area is dimly lit, with emergency lights flickering and security bots patrolling. The station is in lockdown after a catastrophic event, and the AI systems are hostile.

## Main Objectives
- Escape the Maintenance Annex and reach the Reactor Control Room.
- Find a keycard to unlock security doors.
- Restore power to critical systems.
- Survive waves of hostile bots and environmental hazards.

## Quests
1. **Find the Keycard**
   - Search storage rooms and defeat patrolling bots to obtain a keycard.
2. **Restore Power**
   - Locate the power terminal and solve a simple puzzle to restore power to doors and lights.
3. **Reach Reactor Control**
   - Use the keycard to access the control room. Defend against a wave of bots while the reactor is stabilized.
4. **Optional: Rescue a Trapped Worker Bot**
   - Find and free a friendly maintenance bot for a reward (health or upgrade).

## Enemies
- Patrol Bots (melee/ranged)
- Security Drones (flying, light attacks)
- Environmental hazards: electrified floors, steam vents

## Key Items
- Keycard (progression)
- Health packs
- Upgrade module (optional, for rescuing worker bot)

## Environmental Storytelling
- Scattered logs and terminals hint at the station’s downfall and the MindChip’s dark legacy.
- Visual cues: damaged walls, flickering lights, broken bots, warning signs.

## Exit
- After stabilizing the reactor, a new route opens to the next sector of the station.
